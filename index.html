About Green Spear

Green Spear Environmental Protection Organization was founded on June 5, 2010 (World Environment Day), initiated by a group of environmental experts, public welfare activists and young volunteers with ecological dreams, and its headquarters is located in Hangzhou, China. Named "Green Spear", the organization symbolizes using sharp, firm and powerful actions to break through the barriers of environmental pollution, cut off the chains of ecological destruction, and build a solid green defense line for the earth home.

Over the past 14 years, Green Spear has always adhered to the philosophy of "Scientific Environmental Protection, Universal Participation, and Perseverance for Long-term Results". Its business scope covers six major areas: mountain forest vegetation restoration, water resource protection, marine garbage cleaning, environmental science popularization, low-carbon lifestyle promotion, and endangered species protection. It has established branch offices in 31 provinces, municipalities directly under the Central Government and autonomous regions across the country, with more than 200 core staff and over 1.2 million registered volunteers.

As of December 2024, Green Spear has completed a total of 1.2 million mu of afforestation, cleaned more than 5,000 tons of garbage from rivers and lakes, cleared over 800 tons of marine beach garbage, carried out more than 10,000 environmental science popularization lectures, and promoted the green transformation of more than 1,000 enterprises. It has successively won many honors such as "National Advanced Collective in Environmental Protection", "China's Public Welfare and Charity Benchmark Organization", and "Global Pioneer in Ecological Conservation".

Mountain Forest Conservation

After 2 Years, Green Spear Successfully Completes the 100,000 Mu "Sand Control and Greening" Afforestation Project in the Northwest Desert

On November 30, 2024, Green Spear's northwest desert afforestation project came to a successful conclusion. Launched in October 2022, the project invested 120 million yuan and organized 25,000 volunteer person-times. It planted 8 million drought-tolerant plants such as Haloxylon ammodendron, sea buckthorn and Caragana korshinskii in the desert area of Alxa Left Banner, Inner Mongolia, successfully completing 100,000 mu of desert greening, effectively curbing the spread of desertification in the region, and reducing the incidence of sand and dust disasters in 3 surrounding villages by 70%...

Honors & Awards

Triumphant Return! Green Spear Wins the UN "Global Excellence in Ecological Conservation Award", the First Chinese Civilian Environmental Organization to Receive This Honor

On October 15, 2024, the United Nations Environment Programme held the Global Ecological Conservation Awards Ceremony in Nairobi, Kenya. Green Spear Environmental Protection Organization won the "Global Excellence in Ecological Conservation Award" for its outstanding contributions in desert governance and water resource protection. This is the first time the award has been given to a Chinese civilian environmental organization since its establishment. In his award speech, the Executive Director of the United Nations Environment Programme praised: "With 14 years of perseverance, Green Spear has provided a replicable and promotable Chinese solution for global ecological conservation..."

Water Resource Governance

Guard the Mother River! Green Spear Launches the 2024 Yangtze River Basin "Floating Garbage Cleaning and Water Protection" Special Campaign, Having Cleared 1,200 Tons of Garbage So Far

On September 1, 2024, Green Spear's "Floating Garbage Cleaning & Water Protection - Guarding the Yangtze River" special campaign was officially launched in Wuhan, Hubei. This campaign covers 10 provinces and cities in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, with a 5,000-kilometer river section. It has organized 30 professional floating garbage cleaning teams, 18,000 volunteer person-times, and equipped with 50 professional floating garbage cleaning vessels. Up to now, it has cleaned a total of 1,200 tons of surface garbage and aquatic plants in the Yangtze River Basin, improved the excellent water quality rate along the route by 15%, and significantly improved the living environment of nearly 100 species of aquatic organisms...

Marine Environmental Protection

Blue Guardian! Green Spear's 2024 Marine Beach Cleaning Project Concludes, Having Cleared 350 Tons of Coastal Garbage and Protected Marine Biological Habitats

On August 20, 2024, Green Spear's marine environmental protection project was successfully concluded in Xiamen, Fujian. Launched in May this year, the project has carried out garbage cleaning activities in 20 key beach areas in the four major sea areas of the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, East China Sea and South China Sea, organizing 30,000 volunteer person-times, and cleaning a total of 350 tons of coastal garbage such as plastic waste and fishing nets. It has successfully restored 1,500 mu of marine biological habitats, and the occurrence frequency of endangered marine organisms such as lancelets and Chinese white dolphins in the local area has increased by 30%...
